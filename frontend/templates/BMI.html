What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated as: Weight (kg) ÷ Height (m)²

Why BMI Matters

BMI is a screening tool for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

Health Guidelines

Maintaining a healthy BMI can reduce risk of chronic diseases and improve overall wellness.
